Time required: 30 minutes

Monitoring is all about how you can work systematically to maintain and improve your employees' performance. It's about having a good idea of ​​what they do and achieve in their work. Make it a habit to collect information about how your employees perform their tasks. Below we present four different methods which you can learn to develop concrete examples of how you can monitor your employees. When you are done, you can print your own examples.

Method 1: Remove sample from a person's work

This is one of the most effective methods to monitor anyone. This means observing something that the person has worked with.
