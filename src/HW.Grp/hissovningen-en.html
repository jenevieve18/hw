Duration: 10 seconds - 5 minutes

A good way for a manager to bring employee commitment is to succinctly describe what you do in the department associated with the company's vision and goals. When employees understand better the value of what they do. Hone your ability to 10-15 seconds to make a short presentation describing the goal of your business and why you are doing is important to the company and your customers; just enough to do in an elevator.

Answer the following questions in preparation:

Start the clock

1. What is unique about you contribute?

2. In what ways is it important for your customers and for the company?

3. What is your unique skills that make this possible?
