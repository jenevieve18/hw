An effective way to increase productivity is to create a strategic plan for your employees' development. Such a plan involves clarifying a common thread between vision, mission and staff duty. Based on this, one can examine how well the employees' skills match the tasks to be performed. "Hatches" show which items need to be included in employee development.

If employees do not perform their duties in the manner you expect, there may be other than lack of skills. It can also be about things that unclear expectations and tools that are inappropriate, etc. You know what duties the employee will lead to? That is to say, the objectives are clear and tasks clearly defined? Have the employee the tools, instructions and resources needed to successfully carry out their duties? Are employees sufficiently motivated for their job or are there other tasks that collide? Followed tasks of the negative consequences for the employee?
